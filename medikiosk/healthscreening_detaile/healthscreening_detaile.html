Hospitals in Busan

Inje University, Haeundae PAIK Hospital (Health Promotion Center)

Haeundae Paik Hospital General Examination Center provides the best medical services by the best medical staff based on "Baek Hospital's tradition and know-how."
Our Haeundae Paik Hospital General Examination Center is equipped with the best medical staff and the latest medical equipment in an independent and pleasant environment to conduct rapid and accurate medical examinations.
In addition, more accurate and detailed health examinations are possible by introducing state-of-the-art medical information systems such as medical imaging information systems (PACS), prescription delivery systems (OCS), electronic medical records (EMR), and clinical information systems (LIS).
The General Examination Center operates a specialized precision health examination program based on the basic comprehensive health examination program tailored to the daily life of busy modern people, and operates precision health examination programs such as silver and gold for VIP customers.
