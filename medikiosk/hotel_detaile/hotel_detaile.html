Lotte Hotel Seoul (Five Stars)

Lotte Hotel Seoul, the nation's top luxury business hotel, is located in Sogong-dong, Seoul, boasting 1,015 rooms. It boasts excellent access to Seoul's central tourist attractions such as Myeong-dong, Eulji-ro, and Cheonggyecheon, making it the best location for Seoul tourism. Designed by four world-class interior companies, the original interior rooms reflect the latest trends, and you can feel the elegance and refinement on the club floor for a successful business. In addition, Lotte Hotel Seoul provides the best facilities for business events such as warm and luxurious family gatherings, luxury weddings, and large-scale international conferences, and the best restaurants are also the best choice for VIP customers such as state guests.
